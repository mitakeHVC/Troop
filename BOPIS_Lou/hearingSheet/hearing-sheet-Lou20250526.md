## 🎯 プロジェクト概要

### 目的と背景
1. このプロジェクトの目的は何ですか？
Allow users to purchase items from the commodity of their house or mobile device, and then be able to efficiently pick them up at a venue at a later date. Avoiding the necessity of paying for a shipping fee, or arranging delivery

2. プロジェクトの背景を教えてください。
Several factors get into it, but the overall costs related to shipping can be lowered, as well as the customer satisfaction (this is a possibility, not mandatory). So providing varied delivery options will help increase sales

3. 解決したい課題は何ですか？
   - 現状の問題点： Customer acquisition by expanding options, Logistics costs by consolidation and manual operation
   - 問題が発生している原因： As more marketplaces emerge, it is important to maintain a balance between costs and customer satisfaction
   - 問題によって生じている影響： Not sure

4. なぜ今この問題を解決する必要があるのですか？
To expand the reach of the business and capture a bigger market share

### 期待する成果
1. このプロジェクトで期待する成果は何ですか？
Increase in the number of orders due to expanded delivery options
Decrease in delivery costs (though not substantial)
